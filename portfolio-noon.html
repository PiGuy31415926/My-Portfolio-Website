The Noon Whistle Experiment

...a revelation

While attempting to do homework sometime into my freshman year of high school, I noticed something odd: the noon whistle of my town did not sound at noon.

I eventually began collecting data until I learned how to do something with it, which did not happen until my junior year. I was able to prove that the noon whistle was not, in fact, a noon whistle, and now, it has been fixed.
