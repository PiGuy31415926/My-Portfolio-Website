Solo & Ensemble

My journey

Starting solo & ensemble during my freshman year of high school, I found it a good way to challenge myself musically.

My freshman year, I received a 1, the highest mark at any solo & ensemble event, but was not able to compete at state solo & ensemble due to COVID-19. My sophomore year, I achieved the same mark at district s & e and chose not to perform at states because it was virtual with little upsides. Last year, my junior year, I achieved a 1 at district and state solo & ensembles and was nominated and selected for All-Star Band.
